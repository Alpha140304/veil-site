Veil

A private teleprompter for macOS that scrolls as you speak — and stays invisible on screen-shared calls and recordings.

What it does

  • Voice-following scroll — the text keeps pace as you talk, on-device.
  • Invisible to screen sharing, recording and screenshots. Only you see it.
  • Notch mode docks it right under your camera, so you read into the lens.
  • Everything stays on your Mac — no account, no cloud, no tracking.

Privacy in one line

Veil collects no data. Your microphone audio and your scripts never leave your device. Read the full Privacy Policy.
