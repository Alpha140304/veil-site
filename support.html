Frequently asked

The prompter doesn’t follow my voice.

Make sure you granted Microphone and Speech Recognition permission, and that Voice mode is selected. The “heard: …” indicator shows the live transcript — if it’s empty, check macOS System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone / Speech Recognition. Auto and Manual modes work without any permission.

Others can still see the prompter on a call.

Invisibility is built in and on by default. The window is hidden from screen sharing, recording and screenshots — only you see it on your physical display. You can toggle it in the control panel.

Which languages does voice-following support?

15 dictation languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Chinese, Japanese and Korean. Recognition runs on-device where available, with an Apple server fallback unless you enable strict on-device-only mode.

Does Veil send my scripts anywhere?

No. Everything stays on your Mac. See the Privacy Policy.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later, Apple Silicon or Intel.
