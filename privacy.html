Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Short version: Veil collects nothing. Your scripts never leave your Mac, and speech is recognised on-device whenever a model is available (with a strict offline mode). There is no account, no server of ours, and no analytics.

Data we collect

None. Veil does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. The app makes no network requests of its own.

Microphone & speech recognition

In Voice mode, Veil uses Apple’s Speech framework to follow your place in the script. It prefers on-device recognition, so your audio stays on your Mac. If your chosen language has no on-device model, recognition falls back to Apple’s speech service to transcribe — unless you enable the strict “on-device only” mode, which disables that fallback entirely and keeps everything offline. Veil itself never records, stores, or sends your audio to any server of ours; any transcription that does occur is handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Your scripts & settings

The text you write and your preferences are stored locally in the app’s own sandbox container on your Mac. They are not uploaded anywhere. You can export or delete them at any time.

Purchases

Veil Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple. Payment is handled by the App Store; Veil never sees your payment details. We do not receive any personal information through the purchase. Apple’s handling of your purchase is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Third parties

Veil includes no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Veil does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
